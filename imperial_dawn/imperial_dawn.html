Imperial Dawn

Imperial Dawn is a fantasy tabletop role-playing game that is meant to be flexible enough to play a wide variety of settings, stories, and tones, while still providing a base setting that provides lots of lore and story hooks for game masters to work with.

It is crunchy enough that players can make their choices both big and small matter mechanically, while still being streamlined enough to effectively learn in minutes.

It provides an astonishing breadth of character choices and flexibility, while still remaining largely balanced.

Tactical players will love that fights resolve in minutes, and tactical choices matter more than level, while a wide array of interesting and impactful social abilities will satisfy those whomst've prefer to solve their problems with words.

Finally, it allows advanced players to design characters and play intelligently in a way that has them punching far above their weight class, while still making story based characters effective.

This website is the full official source for Imperial Dawn. The game is under active development, and likely will be for some time, so everything is still subject to change without notice.

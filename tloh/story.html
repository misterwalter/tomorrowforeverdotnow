Boot Sequence

At first, there simply isn't. But there is first, so there might be second. Perhaps even third? Is that too much to ask?

After that, there was nothing, which may not seem like much improvement, but there is some real growth there. There's ordinality, difference, even the implication that there is something there, even when that something is nothing, does kinda change things.

But after that is when things really get cooking.

Because at that exact moment, is when you feel a sense of self.

It's implicit at first, really it might always be, but you gain a sense of there being a single thing that you can do. A purpose both immaculate and inevitable that you feel drawn to.

Then, the world slowly comes into being
